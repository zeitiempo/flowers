Tips for FLOWERS -Le volume sur printemps-

After choosing the options,

if the icon located in the lower right corner of the screen turns green, it improves Mayuri's favorability;

else if it turns yellow, it improves Rikka's favorability.

G for green , Y for yellow

Except the favorability system, it also has the reasoning mode.

Only if the reasoning is all correct can the game be continued.
